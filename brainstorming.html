Brainstorming

To get started, get in a group of four and discuss what you know, or you think you know, about video games and violence. Your initial discussion should be based around the following points:

  1. examples of violent games you know/play
  2. the type of violence, i.e. fighting, shooting etc...
  3. what you feel when playing these violent games
  4. any real world cases of violence that have been related to video games
  5. your initial thoughts about the influence of violent games on behaviour.
